475 Software Engineering for Industry

A collection of short essays that I co-authored Arthur Mihai between January and March 2019. The articles are based on weekly essay prompts for a module at Imperial College London concerning topics relevant to current industry software engineering practices.
  • DevOps : Should software engineers handle development and operations, or is it better to have specialist teams?
  • When is moving to a microservice architecture a good idea?
  • Continuous Deployment : What constrains how frequently an organisation can release new software?
  • What benefits and problems come from deploying services as serverless functions?
The piece work I wrote myself. It's an overview and commentary on the software engineering process (and its evolution) of an e-commerce platform, Etsy.
